Overview

Simple operation

Hand-held stock control software. TouchStock counts stock, scans barcodes, checks deliveries and generates labels. It syncs with TouchOffice Web+ to create orders and update stock quantities.

TouchStock handles data effortlessly, enabling you to carry out a partial stock-take whenever you want. There's no need to check the store because you'll be able to check one line of products instead.

01

Deliveries

TouchStock scans everything, item-by-item. Count up what you have and key in the total, then connect TouchStock to TouchOffice Web+ for a no-effort tally on what's been sold.

02

Wastage

When your order arrives TouchStock gives you the power to accurately, quickly and reliably check deliveries. Link TouchStock with TouchOffice Web to update your stock.

03

Quicker stock take

At the end of the day, quickly scan an item and enter the quantity for wastage to update your stock levels.
