About Prime-EPOS

We're the team that
actually shows up.

Based in South Yorkshire. Serving businesses across the UK. We've built our reputation on doing the right thing — proper hardware, honest advice, and support that's there when you need it.

Our story

Built on doing
things properly.

Prime-EPOS was founded on a simple frustration: too many businesses were being sold cheap, underpowered hardware and left without the support they needed when things went wrong. We set out to fix that.

We supply only commercial-grade, POS-ready equipment — the kind that's built for the demands of a real working business, not a home office. Everything we install is configured and tested before it arrives on site.

"We're not just selling you a till. We're taking on responsibility for a critical part of your business — and we take that seriously."

We're proudly based in South Yorkshire, and while we work with businesses across the entire UK, we bring the same local dedication and personal service to every single customer, wherever they are.

What we stand for

Our values aren't
on a poster. They're in our work.

01
Honesty first

We'll tell you what you actually need — not what earns us the most. If a simpler solution does the job, we'll recommend it. That's how we build relationships that last.

02
Hardware that earns its place

We refuse to supply hardware that isn't up to the job. Every terminal, printer, and peripheral we sell is commercial-grade and chosen because it will last in a working business environment.

03
Support you can rely on

When your till goes down on a Friday night, you need someone to pick up. Our UK support team is available around the clock — no voicemail, no ticketing system, no waiting until Monday.

04
Long-term thinking

We're not interested in quick sales. We want to be the EPoS partner you still call in five years. That means getting the setup right from day one and being there as your business grows.
